It's all about perspective

Consulting

In addition to embedded, full-time roles: I offer consulting to founders and teams. Common problems I can help with include:

  • Connecting your brand and product
  • Improving design process
  • Achieving accessibility compliance
  • Mitigating product risks
  • Mentoring young designers

Remote consultations

Typically ~1 hour sessions, once per week.

Remote video sessions that come in several flavors:
Critique: in which you can get critique or second opinions on your brand, design system, prototypes, feature concepts or existing app.
Counsel: in which I act as a sounding board for your design process or investments in design hiring.

Process workshops

Typically ~3 hour sessions, once per month.

In many organizations, teams struggle to generate effective output because they're too focused on output. Team members often believe different aspects of a process have more value but don't often have a forum or tools to express this. I offer in-house workshops to help uncover these individually-held beliefs and foster healthy dialogue within product teams to yield greater productivity and solution quality.

Tactical execution

Remote sessions, timing variable.

Sometimes you just don't have a designer and you need someone to take a pass at the wireframes your [engineer, CTO, intern] built for you. I occasionally have bandwidth for this work, and If I don't, I'm happy to connect you with people who do.
